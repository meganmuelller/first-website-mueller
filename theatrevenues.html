The Seven Best Theatre Venues in Missouri

1. The Fabulous Fox

Without a doubt, The Fox has a rich and unique history to it that brings out it’s beautiful details all around. From the giant glass chandelier in the theatre to the elephant carpet, there is detail everywhere.

(314) 534-1111

2. The Muny

What better way to spend a gorgeous summer day than outside at The Muny in St. Louis? Right in the beautiful Forest Park, visitors can enjoy live productions outside.

(314) 361-1900

3. Missouri Theatre

St. Louis isn’t the only city with gorgeous theatres! Right in the heart of Columbia is the Missouri Theatre. Just like The Fabulous Fox, there are hidden gems throughout the Missouri Theatrre that make it an excellent place to see a show or concert.

(573) 555-2424

4. Starlight Theatre

Starlight Theatre is another outdoor venue presenting Broadway shows and concerts, while remaining one of the only major self-producing outdoor theatres in the U.S.

(816) 363-7827

5. Kauffman Center for the Performing Arts

Through diverse performing arts experiences, the Kauffman Center for the performing arts is for theatre but also many other events to enrich the community.

(816) 994-7222

6. Powell Hall

Home of the St. Louis Symphony, the location features a beautiful white stage with details covering the ceilings, making it an experience and place to remember.

(314) 534-1700

7. The Blue Note

While The Blue Note may not be a Broadway theatre, it certainly has a charm to it that does make it a favorite for celebrities to stop to.

(573) 874-1944
